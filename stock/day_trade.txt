#============== Intra Day  Trading 5 Minutes Timeframe ( New York Session ) ==============
1) Start Trading at 9:10 PM (New York)
2) The trade can be placed in between 9:00PM to 10:20PM.
3) Find out the "Stop Lose(SL)" within that day, the "Stop loss" could be:
   a) The "Stop Loss" can not be cover by any candle.
   b) The "Stop loss" are "Double Top, Double Bottom, or Breakout in the same day, 
      meaning the time range is between 8AM to 9PM.
   c) If that is no any "Stop Loss" within the same day, look for the previous day.
   d) The "Stop Loss" can only be the same day or previous day.
   e) Draw a horizontal line of the "Stop Loss"
4) When don't trade:
   a) When that is the red "Forex News" in the Forex Calendar, and wipe out all the
      "Stop Loss", then no need to trade.
   b) The "Forex News" is in between 8:00PM to 9:00 PM.
   c) No "Stop Loss" found in the day.
5) The Trade MUST be closed before 10:50PM.
   a) If the trade hit the Profit Target before 10:50PM, then close it.
6) When can enter the Trade? Swith to :
   a) Wait for the Signal to occurs,
   b) The Signal could be the Breakout, Double Bottom or Double Top retest, and reversal.
   c) Multiple 5 minutes candle breakout
